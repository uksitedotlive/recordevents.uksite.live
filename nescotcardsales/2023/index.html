Nescot Business & IT Level 1.5 Card sales

The Nescot Business & IT Level 1.5 students conducted card sales at the Skills Hub and outside the Learning Resource Center (LRC) on Monday, as well as participated in an opening evening event during the Nescot open evening for prospective students on Tuesday. It's worth noting that all proceeds generated from these activities were dedicated to supporting the Children's Trust charity in Tadworth.
